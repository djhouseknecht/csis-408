How to play

Goal

Use your paddle to hit the ball towards the AI player. The AI will attempt to return the ball. Whoever can get the ball past the opponents paddle, will score a point.

Instructions

  1. Press the UP ARROW on your keyboard to move your paddle up
  2. Press the DOWN ARROW on your keyboard to move your paddle down
  3. TIP: If you hit the ball at a sharp angle on the edge of your paddle, you will "smash" the ball causing it to increase its speed for a short while
  4. To restart the game, simply refresh your browser
